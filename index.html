enCal

node.js

As students, we find ourselves bombarded with emails about events happening on campus. There are so many club mailing list threads, interview requests, and free food announcements in our inboxes that it's impossible to keep track of all upcoming events efficiently. We wanted to make it quick and easy to prioritize your events while maintaining a clean workspace.

We use natural language processing to automatically extract your event's date, time, and location, and send it - in real-time - to your event management dashboard. You'll get a notification directly in your browser so you can manage your events efficiently while you're surfing the web.

Our integration with SendGrid's Parse Webhook makes it even easier to quickly add events to your calendar without having to fumble around with calendar apps and forms.
